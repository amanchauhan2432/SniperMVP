# Sniper MVP

Welcome to Sniper MVP, a tactical first-person sniper experience developed using Unreal Engine 5 for Android. In this game, players take on the role of a sniper positioned on a distant tower, carefully tracking targets, compensating for environmental factors, and executing precision shots.

With realistic projectile physics, dynamic enemy AI, and immersive scope mechanics, Sniper MVP delivers a focused and intense shooting experience on mobile devices.

# Gameplay

In Sniper MVP, the player remains stationed at a fixed sniper tower with the ability to freely look around and engage enemies from long distances. Using a sniper rifle, players must account for wind direction, bullet travel time, and breathing stability to land accurate shots.

Enemies move intelligently through the environment using AI behaviors such as patrolling, taking cover, and attacking. A successful shot triggers a cinematic slow-motion bullet camera that follows the projectile until impact.

Players earn points based on shot accuracy (headshots and body shots), while managing their health and maintaining precision under pressure.

# Features
- Realistic Sniper Mechanics
  Experience immersive sniping with scope zoom, breath control, and precision shooting mechanics.
- Physics-Based Ballistics
  Bullets are affected by distance and wind direction/strength, requiring skill and timing for accurate shots.
- Dynamic Scope System
  Includes scope in/out, adjustable zoom levels, and real-time wind indicators.
- Focus & Breathing System
  Hold breath for improved accuracy, but manage stamina carefully to avoid heavy breathing penalties.
- Cinematic Bullet Camera
  Watch your successful shots in slow motion as the camera follows the bullet to its target.
- Advanced Enemy AI
  Enemies use Behavior Trees, Blackboards, and EQS for intelligent actions: Idle, Patrol, Alert, Attack, Take Cover and Re-engage
- Interactive UI (Mobile-Friendly) : Scope button, Fire button, Night vision toggle, Focus (breath control) bar, Zoom controls, Wind direction & strength indicators
- Scoring System
  Earn points based on shot accuracy : Headshot: +10 and Body shot: +5. Includes animated feedback text.
- Health & Damage System
  Player has a health bar, and enemies react realistically to hits with ragdoll physics on death.
- Immersive Audio
  Includes firing sounds, bullet impacts, breathing effects, and fatigue feedback.
